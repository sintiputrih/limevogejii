Who is Nikki Mudarris?

MissNikkiiBaby aka Nikki Mudarris is an American television star as well as an entrepreneur and model. She is best known for the reality television show ‘Love & Hip Hop Hollywood’.

She also manages Hollywood and Las Vegas clubs which her family owns.

MissNikkiiBaby: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Blonde and beautiful Nikki Mudarris is 30 years old. She was born and raised in Los Angeles, California on  June 28, 1990. She has a mixed(Morrocan, Italian, and Arabic) ethnicity and she is of American nationality.

Her father’s name is Abu Mudarris and her mother’s name is Marcelle Mudarris. She refers to her father as ‘The Godfather of Burlesque’ and her family was engaged in the nightclubs entertainment industry for decades.

Nikki Mudarris has one sister Monique and one elder brother Omar but unfortunately, her brother passed away in 2015.
