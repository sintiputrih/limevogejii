Katie Flood finally shares iconic Winter House chalkboard that fans have been dying to see Wed

Katie Flood shares behind-the-scenes snaps from her snowy vacation with Winter House fans this November. Some of the details revealed in her Instagram post are what fans have been waiting for. Thankfully, it’s not more make-out scenes from season 3.

Vanderpump Rules star Tom, 41, and Below Deck’s Katie, 31, have viewers talking when it comes to their on-screen romance this year. As Winter House progresses, things get more confusing between the pair after they kiss. Tom Schwartz starts to refrain from any kind of development with Katie – because she has the same name as his ex-wife, Katie Maloney.

Katie Flood shares Winter House detail

In an Instagram post captioned: “Snow rides, flip cup, Bollywood and no fur Kory are all in tonight’s ep of Winterhouse,” Katie is showing her fans just that.

The Below Deck star posted snaps of herself and co-stars horse-riding, playing pool, and much more while filming Winter House.

Katie also shared a snap of the house’s chalkboard which reads: “Grocery List: New personalities, get tattoos, no whiskey, no fur for Kory!”

Some Bravo fans commented that they loved to see her as a “Colorado cowgirl,” while others agreed that fur isn’t “for Kory.”

Another said that Katie’s post had cleared something up for them: “Thank you for posting the chalkboard bc I always try and read it.”

Winter House fans’ ‘jump scare’

While Katie’s BTS Instagram post doesn’t highlight much to do with her romance with Tom Schwartz, it appears to be all fans can talk about on X (Twitter).

One person commented they had a fright when they found out Tom’s age: “This show would be better if i wasn’t watching a bunch of mid 30s and 40 year olds and it was all early twenties. Hearing Katie call Schwartz 40 was a jump scare. Enough.”

Another wrote: “Omg Katie stop! You look desperate.”

However, some left positive comments about their relationship: “I like Tom and this Katie together.”

Katie and Tom are friendly nowadays

Nowadays it appears that Tom and Katie are still friends following their time on Winter House.

Speaking on Katie and Malia’s 30, Flirty, And Flying podcast interview, Katie said Tom is a “hopeless romantic” and a “teddy bear.”

She also admitted she “got s***” from people for not knowing who Tom was. He replied that he “knew” who she was ahead of Winter House.
