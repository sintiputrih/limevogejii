Brooke Shields: I'll Look Like &quot;the Joker&quot; if I Get More Surgery

After trying Botox, Brooke Shields says she is leery about undergoing any more plastic surgery.

"I want laser treatment because I'm not a fan of my wrinkles," she says in the April issue of Ladies' Home Journal. "But I have to find someone with a light touch. I'm scared I'll end up looking like the Joker."

At 44, Shields says she is struggling to find balance in her life. "I don't know if you ever do feel balanced," she says. "People who say they do are lying." The actress – who headlines the family comedy Furry Vengeance (out Apr. 2) – says she feels "greedy" because she wants it all.

"I want to be able to do the projects I want to do and be the kind of mother my daughters want," says Shields, who has two daughters with her writer-husband Chris Henchy – Rowan, 6, and Grier, 3.
