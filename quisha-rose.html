Quisha Rose – Rise to Fame

Quisha Rose is a YouTube star who is best known for uploading makeup tutorials and tricks, beauty tips, product reviews, her life experiences, comedy videos, and travel vlogs on her channel ‘Quisha’. She set up her channel on January 8, 2014, and posted her first video there in May 2017. Since then, she has amassed several thousands of subscribers and nearly two million views on the channel.

Moreover, the oldest video there was posted on May 21, 2017. It was titled ‘Meeting Ariana Grande (Experience) + Vlog’, and it has garnered over 90K views to date.  As the title suggests, the video documents her experience of meeting pop star Ariana Grande. It is a long video, with a runtime of over 45 minutes. In it, she also makes references to the videos she had created previously. However, she has since deleted them.

The most popular video on her channel is ‘Get Ready with Me to Work in London’s Biggest night club’ which has accumulated a decent number of views. A collaborative effort with fellow YouTube star and social media influencer, Jacob White, the video was posted in July 2018.

Some of the other popular videos on her channel are ‘Exposing Our Snapchat Memories,’ ‘Exposing My Private Instagram (Sorry Mum)’, and ‘I Spent £300 On Honeybum! Is It Worth It?’ and ‘Paranormal Activity Caught On Camera: Ouija Board Experiment w/ Touchdalight’.
