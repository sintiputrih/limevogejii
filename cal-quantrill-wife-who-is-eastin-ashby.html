Eastin Ashby was rated as the fourth sexiest daughter of an MLB player by The Sportster in 2016.

During the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, Eastin began volunteering with a group called Letters to Foster Children.

It’s an initiative that, as the name implies, allows people to send letters to the 400,000-plus children in foster care systems across the country to help brighten their days.

Eastin has quickly become the executive director of the programme chapters in Phoenix and Cleveland as a result of her enthusiasm for the initiative.
