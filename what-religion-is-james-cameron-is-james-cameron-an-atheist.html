What religion is James Cameron? Is James Cameron an atheist?

James Cameron is a Canadian filmmaker who was born on August 16, 1954.

He is well known for his science fiction and epic films, and he rose to prominence with The Terminator. He followed up with equally super successful sci-fi films such as Aliens, The Abyss, Terminator 2: Judgment Day, and the action comedy True Lies.

What religion is James Cameron? Is James Cameron an atheist?

Cameron is most definitely an atheist and he doesn’t shy away from it. According to him he converted to Agnosticism but found it “cowardly” so he went back to being a staunch atheist.

In a post on Reddit over a decade ago, he stated: “I’ve sworn off agnosticism, which I now call cowardly atheism. I’ve come to the position that in the complete absence of any supporting data whatsoever for the persistence of the individual in some spiritual form, it is necessary to operate under the provisional conclusion that there is no afterlife and then be ready to amend that if I find out otherwise.”
