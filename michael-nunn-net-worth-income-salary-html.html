Michael John Nunn (born April 14, 1963 in Davenport, Iowa) is a retired American boxer who competed in the amateur ranks. Nunn was a 6′ 2″ southpaw who was the Lineal/IBF middleweight champion as well as the Lineal/WBA super-middleweight champion. He was also the Lineal/IBF super-middleweight champion. In 2004, he was found guilty of narcotics trafficking and sentenced to 24 years in jail.
