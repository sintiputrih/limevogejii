Did Audrey and Mariah Brown get married? Are Audrey and Mariah still together?

Mariah Brown is the only daughter of reality TV personalities Kody Brown and Meri Brown.

She graduated from Loyola University in Chicago in 2020 with a degree in social work.

Mariah currently works as a program manager at Promise South Salt Lake and a recreational assistant at William E. Christoffersen Salt Lake Veterans Home.

She is engaged to Graphic designer Audrey Kriss.

Did Audrey and Mariah Brown get married?

SISTER Wives star Mariah Brown’s partner Audrey Kriss revealed that they are transgender in a lovely message to fans.

The couple met while they were both undergraduates at Westminster College in Utah. In 2018, Mariah introduced Audrey to her family, including Kody’s three other wives Janelle, Robyn, and now-ex Christine.

Mariah and Audrey got engaged in March of 2019 after two years of dating. Audrey, got vulnerable with their followers about their sexuality when they took to social media to share the news.

They garnered much support, including love from Meri. It appears the wedding is on hold for now but they still have a lot of love and a lot to celebrate.

Audrey and Mariah often used social media to advocate for social issues including, Black Lives Matter, and LGBTQ+ equality.

Are Audrey and Mariah still together?

Even though the spotlight is squarely on Mariah Brown and Audrey Kriss, and their relationship, it seems as though the two are still going strong.

They haven’t shared any wedding news, so netizens have to assume that the two are still engaged, and probably planning their wedding.
