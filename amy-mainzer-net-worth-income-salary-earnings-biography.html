Amy Mainzer Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Amy Mainzer (born 1974) is an American astronomer who specialises in astrophysical equipment and infrared astronomy. She received her bachelor’s degree in astronomy from the University of California, Berkeley in 1974. The NEOWISE project, which studies minor planets, and the planned Near Earth Object Camera space telescope mission are both being led by her. She is also the Deputy Project Scientist for Wide-field Infrared Survey Explorer and the Principal Investigator for the NEOWISE project. It was on the History Channel’s The Universe that she made a number of cameo appearances. Also included on the Star Trek Generations home video DVD is a documentary film titled “Stellar Cartography: On Earth,” which she appears in (March 2010).
