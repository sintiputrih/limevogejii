Best dressed celebrities in January 2024: Rosamund Pike, Barry Keoghan, Billie Eilish more

Out with the old and in with the new is a usual motto for January, yet last year's style muses continued to prove that outfit repeating and vintage fabrics have never been hotter. 

As the first month of 2024 rolls in, a fleet of best dressed stars have already started their year by proving their sartorial prowess. 

With the start of awards season just around the corner, we have the Golden Globe Awards, the premieres of Priscilla, Argylle, Poor Thing and more highly-anticipated films set to deliver a cocktail of noteworthy red carpet outfits from the likes of Jacob Elordi, Emma Stone and Dua Lipa. 

As we gear up for the stars to saturate our feeds with a series of fabulous outfits, keep scrolling to discover who's made it as best dressed this month.

MORE FASHION

Best dressed celebrities in January 2024

Lindsay Lohan

Lindsay Lohan totally made fetch happen on the pink carpet of the Mean Girls premiere in New York City on Monday 8 January. 

Two decades since the star's original Mean Girls movie came out, Lindsay looked just as glamorous in a waist-baring column dress adorned with a glittering rhinestone belt. 

Gugu Mbatha-Raw

It may only be January, but there's an awful lot of drop-waist silhouettes and tulle floating around 2024. Luckily, we're totally here for the ballerina-inspired trend. 

Looking breathtaking at the premiere of her new Netflix film, Lift, Gugu Mbatha-Raw was angelic in a grey tulle dress and timeless red lip.

Gillian Anderson

Rosamund Pike

Rosamund Pike epitomised ethereal beauty in a drop waist, salmon pink tulle gown at the Vanity Fair and Amazon MGM Studios awards season celebration on 6 January. 

Embracing a ballerina aesthetic, the Saltburn star swept her hair into a slick bun. 

Danielle Brooks

Danielle Brooks was a vision at W Magazine's Annual Best Performances Party in a glittering Champagne-hued gown.

Natasha Lyone

Natasha Lyonne's fiery red hair was her best accessory as she slipped into a satin slip dress for the Vanity Fair and Amazon MGM Studios awards season celebration. The Orange is the New Black star added a delicate necklace and fluttery lashes to complete her 1920's inspired aesthetic. 

Billie Eilish

It may be the first month of the year, but Billie Eilish already has a list of awards and accolades under her belt for her melancholic Barbie ballad 'What Was I Made For?'. 

On 4 January, the star struck a sartorial chord in an oversized trouser suit combo for the 35th Annual Palm Springs International Film Festival Awards Gala at the Convention Center in Palm Springs. 

Barry Keoghan

Saltburn star Barry Keoghan opted for a sheer mesh shirt and slick tailored trousers for the W Magazine's Annual Best Performances Party at Chateau Marmont on 5 January. 

HOT RIGHT NOW

Margot Robbie

Proving Barbiecore still reigns supreme in 2024, Margot Robbie channelled her movie alter-ego in the prettiest pink polka dot dress to attend the 35th Annual Palm Springs International Film Festival Awards Gala. The Australian beauty's retro mini featured 80s-inspired epaulettes, pleating detailing on one hip and a cute rose adorning the bodice. She added peep-toe mules in a matching satin fabric and styled her golden locks in beachy waves.

Taraji P. Henson

The Colour Purple actress Taraji P. Henson caused a stir when she graced the red carpet for the Palm Springs International Film Festival Awards Gala. Lighting up the Convention Center, the 53-year-old Empire star looked gorgeous in a shimmering green gown with a ruched bodice and caped sleeves. A subtle side-split offered a peek at her gold strappy heels and glittering drop earrings completed her head-turning look.

Emma Roberts

Emma Roberts donned a seriously boyfriend coded outfit at the Chicago Bulls v New York Knicks game, teaming relaxed denim jeans with a band tee and oversized striped cardigan.

Ashley Roberts

Wonka fever appears to have hit London, as former Pussycat Dolls star Ashley Roberts stepped out in a chic double denim outfit and futuristic white sunglasses that wouldn't go amiss in a scene of Charlie & The Chocolate Factory.

The star paired her effortless ensemble with a white leather handbag and nude heeled pumps. 

Michelle Keegan

Christina Milian and Matt Pokora

Power couple Christina Milian and Matt Pokora took couple dressing to the next level as they stepped out in Los Angeles in eclectic outfits at the start of the month. 

The raven-haired singer, who sang the iconic noughties soundtrack 'Call Me! Beep Me!' for Disney's Kim Possible, made a case for bold leather as she rocked a scarlet snake-print mini dress and chunky chain heels.

Julia Fox

Of course we could trust Julia Fox to serve up a head-turning look to kickstart 2024. 

The Uncut Gems star stepped out in Miami on the first day of the month in a striking black and yellow ensemble that is giving us major The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift vibes.

Dua Lipa

Dua Lipa looked sublime as she rang in the New Year with a glitzy evening in the Indian city of Jaipur.

The 'Houdini' singer's outfit of choice featured a trailing skirt and a dramatic thigh-split. She teamed the piece with a pair of suede black pointed-toe kitten heels. Dua also carried a leather maroon shoulder bag. 

Nicola Peltz & Brooklyn Beckham

Nicola Peltz and Brooklyn Beckham need no introduction as one of the best dressed power couples in show business. It-Girl Nicola was a fashion muse in a glittering discoball mini dress for her very first outfit of 2024. 

The 28-year-old rocked a semi-sheer mesh mini, adorned with reflective, Art Deco-esque adornments. Her husband Brooklyn kept it casual in a white tee and black jeans.
