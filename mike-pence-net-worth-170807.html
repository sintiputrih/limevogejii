Mike Pence Net Worth

Mike Pence is an American politician who was born on June 7, 1959 in Columbus, IN. He served in the House of Representatives for 10 years before becoming the 50th Governor of Indiana in 2013. Pence is a Republican and is known for his conservative views, particularly his opposition to LGBT rights. He also controversially claimed in 2001 that smoking does not kill. In 2017, he was sworn in as Vice President alongside Donald Trump.
