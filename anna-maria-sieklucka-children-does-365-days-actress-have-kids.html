Anna-Maria Sieklucka children: Does 365 Days actress have kids?

Anna-Maria Sieklucka is an actress known for her role as Laura Biel in 365 Days, a popular 2020 erotic movie. Anna-Maria is Polish.

Sieklucka was born in Lublin, the largest city in eastern Poland. Her father, Jerzy Antoni Sieklucki, is a lawyer. Sieklucka studied at the Wrocław-based Faculty of Puppetry of AST National Academy of Theatre Arts and graduated in 2018.

Anna-Maria Sieklucka children: Does 365 Days actress have kids?

Anna-Maria does not have children yet. She made her film debut playing Laura Biel, opposite Michele Morrone, in the erotic drama film 365 Days (2020) and the sequel. She described filming as a challenge and was initially hesitant to accept the role after reading the script.

The movie was met with very negative critical reception but was popular in many countries all over the world, according to Newsweek, it was the most-watched Netflix film in 2020. Sieklucka was nominated for a Golden Raspberry Award for Worst Actress for her performance.
