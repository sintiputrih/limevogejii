Get To Know Drag Queen Marcia Marcia Marcia! Real Name, Age

RuPaul’s Drag Race Season 15 is already here and this season will be more exciting than ever to watch following some changes the show has made. First and foremost, the show has got its new home MTV (the series’ third network after Logo and VH1). Secondly, the queens have now $200,00 to fight for. Among the 16 contestants competing in Rupaul’s Drag Race Season 15, Marcia Marcia Marcia is one of them. Learn about them in the wiki article below.

Marcia Marcia Marcia On RuPaul’s Drag Race Season 15

Taking to Instagram on December 14, 2022, Marcia shared promotional pictures of RuPaul’s Drag Race Season 15 and announced their participation in the show. They wrote, “I’m ready for the ride of my LIFE! 🙌 #DragRace Season 15 premieres FRIDAY JAN 6 at 8/7c on its new home, @mtv! 🏁”

The newest season of Drag Race kicked off with a two-part premiere on January 6, 2023, at 8 pm, with RuPaul’s Drag Race: Untucked premiering immediately after.

Besides Marcia, the other 15 queens competing for $200,00 in RuPaul’s Drag Race Season 15 are Amethyst, Anetra, Aura Mayari, Irene Dubois, Jax, Loosey Laduca, Luxx Noir London, Malaysia Babydoll Foxx, Mistress Isabelle Brooks, Princess Poppu, Robin Fierce, Salina Estitties, Sasha Colby, Sugar, and Spiece.

As per Marcia’s bio on Eonline, they were the most popular girl in school–and mom’s favorite. Marcia can sing, dance, and even bring Broadway chops to the main stage when required.

In an introduction to themself on RuPaul’s Drag Race YouTube channel, Marcia shared that the first time they ever did drag was when they had put together this number, Break Free by Ariana Grande. Coming off stage, they felt the public reaction was amazing and exhilarating.

Ahead of the show’s premiere, Marcia sat with The Knocturnal and talked about a lot of things. In the interview, they described their drag in 3 words: “demure, colorful, and pretty.”

Marcia also shared that they were on the subway between 59th and 125th and could not answer the phone when they received the call for RuPaul’s Drag Race Season 15. But, they got out immediately on the 125th even though it was not their stop, and called the number back.

In the interview, the NYC ingenue also described what it was like meeting RuPaul for the first time. They revealed RuPaul has a very welcoming presence. They said, “We were all just in the workroom doing whatever and we heard the ‘Hello, hello, hello!’ And it was truly a welcomed scare. A welcome jumpscare.”

Marcia Marcia Marcia Out Of Drag

Out of drag, Marcia Marcia Marcia has naturally brown hair and eyes. According to their September 8, 2022nd Instagram post, they dyed their hair blonde.

Marcia Marcia Marcia Partner

Unfortunately, Marcia never posts anything about their dating life on their social media. That makes it super hard for us to know about their partner.

With that said, the NYC-based drag queen once did reveal their celeb crush. They said it would probably have to be Jeremy Sumpter in Peter Pan.

Is Marcia Marcia Marcia On Instagram?

Marcia Marcia Marcia is on Instagram @marciax3nyc, where they have more than 100 thousand followers. They usually post everything about their performances, costumes, and other general kinds of stuff over there.

Marcia also has 67 thousand followers on their @marciax3nyc Twitter account. You can find them on Twitter as well.

Marcia Marcia Marcia Age

Born in 1996, Marcia Marcia Marcia’s age was 26 at the time of this writing.

Marcia Marcia Marcia Job

Coming to Marcia Marcia Marcia’s job, they are a performer. They are a hit on the NYC club circuit, credit to their hilarious mixed, handmade costumes, and dynamic dance moves.

Their love and talent for theatre have landed them in different sports. They were recently seen Off-Broadway as an Angel in Kinky Boots and in the national tour of Hello, Dolly! starring Carolee Carmello. Their jump split during the final Off-Broadway performance of Kinky Boots also went viral on TikTok.

Marcia told, “For drag queens, New York City is a place where your Broadway dreams go to die and then you become a drag queen because you’re like, ‘Oh I have all this performance experience, I got to put it somewhere,” in her introduction video of RuPaul’s Drag Race Season 15.

The Kinky Boots star makes big money from selling their merchandise. As of January 2023, they had Marcia MTQ Tshirt for $$35, Marcia Signed Print for $20, Marcia Logo Sticker for $20, and Marcia Logo Tote for $25 in store. Marcia also earns a part of their income from Cameo, where their fans pay a certain amount for a personalized video.

What Is Marcia Marcia Marcia Real Name?

Marcia Marcia Marcia’s real name was unavailable as of January 2023. They go by the pronouns they/them.

About Marcia’s drag name origin, they revealed that it is a reference to an iconic scene from “The Brady Bunch”, where the character of Jan gets upset when Marcia Brady gets all the praise at school and repeats Marcia’s name three times – and because “Marcia is the pretty, popular, smart and talented one”.
