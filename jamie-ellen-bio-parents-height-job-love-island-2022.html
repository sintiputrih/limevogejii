Jamie Ellen On Love Island 2022

Love Island never fails to surprise its viewers. They recently introduced four new bombshells to stir up some relationships, hopefully for the last time. One of the single ton who was introduced on 20 July 2022 was Jamie Ellen. Jamie told the producers, “I play football every single day, and in terms of stepping on people’s toes, we are all in there for the same reason, so I wouldn’t have a problem upsetting a few people.”

He added: “I am coming in a bit later, so I probably will upset a few lads, but you gotta do what you gotta do”.

Jamie admitted that he likes to take his time when it comes to falling for someone. He voiced, “I haven’t been in love, I am a really slow-burner, I’ve got a high guard all the time. It takes a lot for that barrier to come down, but once it is down, I am all for it”.

The football player added that he might be a catch because he believes that he is loyal, only had two girlfriends, and is fully committed. According to his Facebook, he joined the show while his relationship status was single.

Jamie Ellen Job

Coming to the show, Jamie Ellen introduced himself as a football player. He signed his pro contract for the first time in 2012.

On 20 July 2022, his football team FC Halifax Town announced on their Twitter page that Jamie decided to join the cast of Love Island 2022 as he shared that the opportunity was very important to him. However, fans of the club weren’t thrilled. One fan wrote, “Get rid of him. He’s supposed to be a professional footballer. STID”. Some of them were a bit personal in the comment section regarding his choices to join the show.

But another person shared a different opinion. He commented, “He will put our great club on the map though !!”

Coming back to his career, Jamie began his football career at Fleetwood Town in 2012 taking in loan spells at Barrow & AFC Fylde. He joined Stalybridge Celtic in 2015 before moving on to Southport where he played in the season 2016-2017. And then he joined Dover Athletic in 2017 for an undisclosed fee. He made his debut for the club on 5 August 2017, in an opening day 1–0 away victory over Hartlepool United in which he scored the game’s only goal.

Jamie signed for FC Halifax Town in July 2019 after canceling his contract by mutual consent. On 9 June 2022, Jamie signed a new contract with the club to take him through to the 2022–23 season.

Donning the No. 7 jersey, Jamie plays as a Forward for FB Halifax. His club said that “the matter will be reviewed on his return”.

In November 2019, Jamie was called up by Montserrat and made his debut on 16 November 2019 which marked his international career.

As for his academic qualification, Jamie is studying at Fulwood Academy.
