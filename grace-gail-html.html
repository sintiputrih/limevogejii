Grace Gail - Age, Family, Bio

Wife of Adam Rodriguez. She worked as a model for several years. Her husband gained fame through his work in the Magic Mike franchise as well as his role in CSI: Miami. She is often photographed at red-carpet events with her husband. 

Before Fame

She and Adam got married on May 2, 2016. 

Trivia

Her father is Max Gail, who earned two Primetime Emmy Awards. She is the daughter of Max and his second wife, Nan. Her father made his acting debut in 1971 in The Organization. 

Family Life

She has an older brother named Maxwell Gail III. She and Adam have three children together. 

Associated With

Channing Tatum was a guest at her wedding.
