The Bahamas Wedding of NFL Star J.J. Watt and Soccer Star Kealia Ohai


The wedding bells have rung for NFL star J.J. Watt and soccer player Kealia Ohai after three years of courtship.

Last Saturday night, the couple exchanged vows at The Ocean Club, a Four Seasons Resort, in the Bahamas.

Following the ceremony, J.J. Watt took to his social media accounts the next day to share numerous photos of their wedding with the soccer pro, captioning it: ‘Best day of my life. Without question.’

In one of the images, the 28-year-old blonde beauty was pictured in a jeweled fishtail gown with dainty straps and diamond-encrusted drop earrings, while 30-year-old Watt opted for a cream suit. 

The newlyweds also shared some playful snapshots donning casual outfits.

A mutual friend, Brian Cushing, who used to play for the Houston Texans with Watt, played a key role in introducing the couple. They obtained a marriage license in Harris County, Texas last week, nine months after their engagement.
