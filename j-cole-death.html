J. Cole is Alive, Rappers Death Rumors Debunked

Several celebrities have fallen prey to death hoaxes in recent years, but J. Cole, an American rapper and record producer, has lately made news for the same reason.

This has happened to a number of A-list celebrities from the entertainment business in the past, including Tom Holland, Oprah Winfrey, Dwayne Johnson, and many others. Continue reading to learn more about J. Cole and the claims that he has died.

Is rapper J. Cole dead or alive?

The reply to the earlier query is a living thing. As a bogus news source made a similar claim, rumours of Jermaine Lamarr Cole’s death began to circulate on social media. The article was titled “J.Cole found dead.”

Breaking News 247 released the news story. For those of you who don’t know, Breaking News 247 is a service that allows users of social media to create phoney postings and upload them to the internet.

Nevertheless, if you read the report carefully, you will notice that the website also has a notice stating that it is satirical. Consider it lightly. This is a joke.

What has J. Cole been working on lately?

According to numerous sources, J. Cole is currently engaged in writing new music for his followers throughout the globe. He recently collaborated on the song “On The Street” with J-Hope from the South Korean boy band BTS.

Besides from this, his music has also been featured on Creed 3’s soundtrack. His song Adonis Interlude (The Montage) has received a tonne of love and appreciation from both reviewers and fans.
