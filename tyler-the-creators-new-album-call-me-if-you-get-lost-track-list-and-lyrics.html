Tyler, the Creators new album Call Me If You Get Lost: Track list and lyrics

TYLER, the Creator took the world by surprise this Friday (25 June, 2021) when he unveiled his new album, Call Me If You Get Lost.

Two years after his Grammy-winning LP IGOR, the rapper is back with features with Lil Wayne, Pharrell, Ty Dolla $ign, Domo Genesis and more.

What is the track listing for Call Me If You Get Lost?

Tyler has spoiled his fans with 16 new tracks :

  • Sir Baudelaire
  • Corso
  • Lemonhead (featuring 42 Dugg)
  • Wusyaname (featuring YoungBoy Never Broke Again and Ty Dolla $ign)
  • Lumberjack
  • Hot Wind Blows (featuring Lil Wayne)
  • Massa
  • Run It Up (featuring Teezo Touchdown)
  • Manifesto (featuring Domo Genesis)
  • Sweet/I Thought You Wanted To Dance (featuring Brent Faiyaz and Fana Hues)
  • Momma Talk
  • Rise! (featuring Daisy World)
  • Blessed
  • Juggernaut (featuring Lil Uzi Vert and Pharrell Williams)
  • Wilshire
  • Safari

Prior to its release, a promotional billboard for the album’s rollout was spotted by fans on 9 June, 2021.

The billboard included the name of the album as well as a phone number.

When called, fans would be confronted with a recorded conversation between Tyler and his mother.

On 15 June, the Love Island fan tweeted the phone number on the billboard, confirming his involvement.

What do the lyrics of Tyler, the Creator’s Wusyaname mean?

The following day, 16 June, Tyler released the first single from the album, Lumberjack, with its traditional music video.

A week later, fans were hit with another single titled Wusyaname and another accompanying video.

The clip begins with Tyler narrating a road trip to an undisclosed location.

Arriving at what seems to be a country club, he spots a woman whom he quickly becomes interested in.

“In California, you spend most of your life in cars, moving, traveling, searching for somewhere to land,” he narrates at the beginning of the clip.

“I don’t mind tiptoeing across the globe for thrills and flying to Vienna to see a show at the Kunsthistorisches Museum or headed south to raise dolphins on jet skis.”

“But this time I ended up in [bleep], lookin’ for this brioche my boy told me about. That’s when I met ‘She.'”

Infatuated with the woman, Tyler raps:

Aw, you look malnourished
Let’s get some bread
Fry the egg yolk and drown it in syrup
You pick a spot, I pick a tail number and we can be tourists.
