Who is Marcus Jordans Girlfriend: Meet Larsa Pippen

Marcus Jordan is an American businessman and former college basketball player who played for the UCF Knights men’s basketball team. He is the second of five children of legendary basketball player and Hall of Famer Michael Jordan.

Who is Larsa Pippen?

Born Larsa Younan on July 6, 1974 in Chicago, Illinois, United States to Assyrian parents. Her mother is Lebanese and her father a Syrian. She has three siblings; Bella Youkhana, Samuel Younan, and Ninos Younan.

She had her basic, high school education in Chicago, Illinois. After high school, Larsa enrolled at the University of Illinois, Chicago where she earned her Bachelor of Arts degree.

In February 2011 when Bravo’s reality series, The Real Housewives of Miami she debuted in its first season. Other cast members included Lea Black, Adriana de Moura, Alexia Nepola, Marysol Patton and Cristy Rice.

After just seven (7) episodes, Larsa departed the show due to her being “too level-headed” for the Miami housewives cast. The show nevertheless ended after three seasons as a result of the declining ratings leading up to the reunion.

The fourth season was premiered in 2021, with Larsa making a return. New faces were added to the cast, including Guerdy Abraira, Kiki Barth, Julia Lemigova and Nicole Martin. Prior to that, Bravo re-ran the first three seasons of the show in 2020.

Over the years, Larsa has also been a part on Keeping Up with the Kardashians. She makes frequent appearances mainly because she is friends with Kim Kardashian and the rest of the Kardashian-Jenner family.

In addition to making appearances of television shows, she launched her own high-end jewelry line called “Larsa Marie”. She modeled her own line which focused on “self-love and empowering women to feel like their best selves”.

Larsa Younan was previously married to her husband and Scottie Pippen in 1997. The couple had a spilt in 2016 and got back together after they reconciled. They again began their separation process again in 2018 and finalized their divorce on December 15, 2021. She is a mother of four.

Currently, she is in a relationship with the Marcus Jordan, the second oldest son of Michael Jordan, legendary basketball player and Hall of Famer Michael Jordan.

Larsa Pippen was born on July 6, 1974 in Chicago, Illinois, United States. She is currently 49 years old (as of 2023). She stands at a height of 5 feet 2 inches tall (1.57 m) and weighs in at 60 kilograms (132 lbs).

Larsa Pippen was born in Chicago, Illinois, United States. The blonde beauty is an American national and her ethnicity is Assyrians. Her grandparents were immigrants from Morocco and her father and mother are from Syria and Lebanon respectively.

Larsa Pippen is an American businesswoman and reality television personality. She is an original cast on Bravo’s reality series “The Real Housewives of Miami”, being a part since its premiere in 2011, taking a hiatus and then rejoining in 2021.
