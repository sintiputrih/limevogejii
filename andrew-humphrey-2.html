Andrew Humphrey is a CBM

Andrew has earned the reputed AMS Certified Broadcast Meteorologist (CBM) distinction; hence, he is referred to as Andrew Humphrey, CBM.

Moreover, he has been named the first and only Detroit meteorologist with two degrees in meteorology.

He received his BSE degree in Meteorology from the University of Michigan and later earned an MS in Meteorology from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), where he wrote his thesis on “The Behavior of the Total Mass of the Atmosphere.”
