John Fogerty Net Worth

John Fogerty is an American singer and guitarist born on May 28, 1945 in Berkeley, CA. He is best known for his work with the band Creedence Clearwater Revival, with hits such as "Fortunate Son" and "Have You Ever Seen The Rain". After the band's dissolution, Fogerty pursued a successful solo career and announced in 2012 that he was writing a memoir about his career.

About

Singer and Guitarist for Creedence Clearwater Revival, best known for hits such as "Fortunate Son" and "Have You Ever Seen The Rain." Fogerty later pursued a successful solo career. Fogerty announced in 2012 that he was writing a memoir about his career. 

Before Fame

He took guitar lessons from Barry Olivier. One of his first bands was called The Blue Velvets.

Trivia

He is listed on Rolling Stone's list of 100 Greatest Guitarists and 100 Greatest Singers.

Family Life

He married Martha Paiz on September 4, 1965; after their divorce, he married Julie Lebiedzinski on April 20, 1991. He has two sons named Shane and Tyler and two daughters named Lyndsay and Kelsy.

Associated With

He was once in The Blue Velvets with Doug Clifford.
