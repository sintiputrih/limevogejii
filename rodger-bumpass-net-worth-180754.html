Rodger Bumpass Net Worth

Rodger Bumpass is an American voice actor born on November 20, 1951 in Jonesboro, AR. He is best known for his role as Squidward Tentacles on the popular animated series SpongeBob SquarePants. He has also provided the voice of Professor Membrane on the animated series Invader Zim.
