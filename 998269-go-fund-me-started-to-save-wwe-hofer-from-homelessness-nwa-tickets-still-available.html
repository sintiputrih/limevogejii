GoFundMe Started To Save WWE HOFer From Homelessness; NWA 70 Tickets Still Available

GoFundMe Started To Save WWE HOFer From Homelessness

A GoFundMe has been set up in an effort to save WWE Hall of Famer Paul Orndorff from losing his home.

Orndorff is in need of $10,000 to pay property taxes or he could face eviction.

The following comes direct from Orndorff’s Go Fund Me page:

After entertaining the world over, Mr Wonderful Paul Orndorff sacrificed his mind and body and is now in desperate financial abyss. He is in need of 10 thousand dollars to pay his property taxes or he will lose his house. We need to hit that goal by October 1st. Thank you for your donation and the largest donation submitted by 10/1 of at least 500 dollars will receive his Mr Wonderul black and gold robe if we hit our 10k dollar goal. God bless.

Readers looking to assist with the efforts to keep Orndorff in his home may donate HERE.
