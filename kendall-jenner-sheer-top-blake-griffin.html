Kendall Jenner Wears Tank Top

Kendall Jenner's hangout streak with NBA star Blake Griffin continues. The latest of the duo's joint sightings was at Malibu over the Labor Day weekend, where they soaked up the heat in 90-degree weather, TMZ reports.

Jenner dressed for the climate in a sheer, cropped white tank top over a floral bathing suit top, cutoff high-waisted jeans, and shades. Griffin, on the other hand, kept covered in a tie-dye tee and jeans.

This follows Jenner's recent outings with Griffin, which included a sushi date at Nobu and some downtime in Montauk last month. Even though the two seem to be getting close, don't expect any exclusivity yet. She's apparently still dating A$AP Rocky.
