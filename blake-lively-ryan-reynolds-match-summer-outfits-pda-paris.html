Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds Match Summer Outfits in Paris

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds took their love—and their cool couple style—to Paris this past weekend.

The actors were seen strolling the city Saturday, holding hands in perfectly coordinating summer looks. Lively looked lovely in a white shirt dress with a lavender floral pattern. Over it she layered not one, but two light trench coats—one beige and the other navy with thin white stripes. She finished the look with beige checkered sneakers worn with white-and-navy crew socks, and Chanel’s shearling mini hot-pink flap bag with a chain strap. Her only jewelry seemed to be tiny gold hoop earrings and her gold wedding band. The Gossip Girl alum wore minimal makeup and had her hair down in a messy side-parted style.

Reynolds also included white, beige, and navy in his outfit. The Deadpool star looked effortlessly cool in a beige button-up with thin blue and red stripes worn open and layered over a scoop-neck white tee. He added a warm tone with a pair of brown pants, and finished the look with a navy baseball hat and white Adidas Samba sneakers with navy stripes worn with light-blue socks. He accessorized with round black-tinted sunglasses, an Apple Watch, and several colorful beaded bracelets.

BACKGRID

Lively and Reynolds—who married in 2012 and now have four children—regularly coordinate looks, both on and off the red carpet. Though they mostly stay out of the spotlight, they were photographed out in New York City this April, looking so in love in (again) matching beige-and-blue outfits.
