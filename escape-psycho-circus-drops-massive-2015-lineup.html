Escape: Psycho Circus Drops Massive 2015 Lineup

Recommended Videos

As festival season draws to a close, you can expect the organizers of Halloween massives to face off for the favor of fall festivalgoers. A couple days ago we brought you the news of HARD Day of the Dead’s supposed lineup leak, and it looks like Insomniac has opted to fire back with a stacked lineup announcement for Escape: Psycho Circus.

Billed as “Escape from Wonderland” or “Escape: All Hallows Eve” in previous editions, the two-day event takes place from October 30th to October 31st – so HARD Day of the Dead attendees could still feasibly attend a day of Escape, or vice versa. While names like Martin Garix, Above & Beyond and DJ Snake give the lineup some main stage mass appeal, many more obscure artists provide a draw for more tasteful dance music enthusiasts.

Amine Edge & Dance returns to the top of Day 2’s agenda, as well as Richie Hawtin’s acclaimed ENTER. experience – and new faces like Ephwurd and Kidnap Kid find themselves on the flyer for the first time.

Now that both lineups have hit the press, we want to know which one better suits your fancy. Check out the lineup for Escape: Psycho Circus below and tell us if you’d rather attend this festival or HARD Day of the Dead in the comments section.
