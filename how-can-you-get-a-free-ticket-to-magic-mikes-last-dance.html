How can you get a free ticket to Magic Mikes Last Dance?

Magic Mike’s Last Dance is one of the month’s most hotly anticipated films. But, if you want to protect your wallet while also enjoying the end of Magic Mike’s story, then you’re in luck. Right now, a buy two get one free ticket promotion is running, which means you can snag a free ticket if you take two friends to see the movie.

What is Magic Mike’s Last Dance about?

The third film in the Magic Mike franchise and the sequel to 2015’s Magic Mike XXL, Magic Mike’s Last Dance, sees Mike Lane (played by Channing Tatum) falling on hard times. Lacking money after a failed business deal, Mike is working bartending jobs, trying to work out how to find stability once more. 

However, when wealthy socialite Maxandra Mendoza gives Mike an offer too good to turn down, he heads to London to try and claw his way back to the top. But Mike soon learns that this offer has a sting in the tail, meaning he’ll need to work harder than ever to earn back his old life. 

How to get a free ticket to Magic Mike’s Last Dance

Currently, Fandango is running a promotion where you can get a third ticket for free when you buy two. 

To claim this offer, head to the Fandango website or log into their mobile app. From there, select your theater and the time you wish to see Magic Mike’s Last Dance and purchase three tickets. When you reach the checkout, enter the promo code MAGICMIKE in the code box and click “Apply” to get the third ticket for free. 

However, there are some caveats to this offer. Firstly, the promotion is only available in America and only applies if you’re seeing the movie at a theater partnered with Fandango. Also, there is a limited number of tickets available as part of this promotion, so it’s best to claim yours sooner rather than later if you and your two best buddies want to see the movie. 

Also, note that the promotion only covers “up to $15 total ticket and convenience fee value.” If your third ticket costs more than this, Fandango’s terms note: “If cost of the ticket (and convenience fee) obtained through the Code is more than maximum value of the Code, then you must pay the difference.”

This promotion ends when all the code redeems have been used when Magic Mike’s Last Dance leaves theaters or on March 31st, 2023.
