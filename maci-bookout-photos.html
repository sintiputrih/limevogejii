Maci Bookout Pics Of Teen Mom Star Hollywood Life

Maci Bookout rocks a white romper to her bachelorette party at 10ak Nightclub in Las Vegas’ Mirage Hotel and Casino on Sept. 3, 2016. She was born and raised in Chattanooga, TN where she still resides. She first gained fame on MTV’s ’16 and Pregnant’ in 2009 when she was pregnant with her son Bentley. Then she and other cast members transitioned into ‘Teen Mom OG’ where fans have followed their stories ever since. Maci and Bentley’s dad Ryan Edwards broke up in 2010 and she is now happily married to Taylor McKinney, who she began dating in 2012. They welcomed their first child, a daughter named Jayde on first child in January 2015 and a year later she gave birth to her third child, a son named Maverick. Maci and Taylor married on October 8, 2016 in a ceremony in Florida. She has appeared on the TV show ‘Naked and Aftraid’ and authored the book ‘I Wasn’t Born Bulletproof: Lessons I’ve Learned (So You Don’t Have To)’ in 2017.
