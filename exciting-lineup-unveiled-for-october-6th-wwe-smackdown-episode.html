Exciting Lineup Unveiled for October 6th WWE SmackDown Episode

WWE SmackDown on FOX is gearing up for an exciting night on October 6th, with a lineup that promises exiting matchups and top-tier wrestling action.

Here’s a look at the updated list of matches and superstars scheduled to appear:

  • Bobby Lashley vs. Rey Mysterio: Two wrestling powerhouses collide as Bobby Lashley takes on the high-flying Rey Mysterio.
  • Austin Theory vs. Dragon Lee: Young sensation Austin Theory faces off against the talented Dragon Lee.
  • Asuka & Charlotte Flair vs. Bayley & IYO SKY: Tag team action takes center stage with a matchup that sees the formidable duo of Asuka and Charlotte Flair teaming up to face the tough pairing of Bayley and IYO SKY.
  • Cody Rhodes, Jey Uso, and the Judgment Day Segment: Cody Rhodes and Jey Uso are expected to carry on their on-going feud with The Judgment Day.

With this lineup, WWE SmackDown on October 6th is shaping up to be an unforgettable night of wrestling action.

Fans can look forward to witnessing their favorite superstars in action and the potential for surprise twists and turns in the ongoing WWE storylines. Don’t miss out on all the excitement!
