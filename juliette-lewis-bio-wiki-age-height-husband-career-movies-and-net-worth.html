Lewis Career

Lewis made her first major screen appearance in 1987 in the television film Home Fires.  She then appeared as Kate Farrell on the television series I Married Dora, which ran from 1987 until 1988. As a voice actress, she made her TV show debut as Paula Wisconsin in the animated series, Free for All, in 2003.

Lewis appeared opposite Benicio del Toro in 2001 in the thriller The Way of the Gun, and appeared as a vocalist on the track “Bad Brother” by the band The Infidels, from The Crow: Salvation soundtrack album. The album was released in April 2011
