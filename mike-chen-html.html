Mike Chen - Age, Family, Bio

Versatile Youtuber who captains numerous popular channels including the insanely popular mystery and paranormal channel Beyond Science and the popular food channels Strictly Dumpling and Pho The Love of Food. He also shares the Asian culture channel The Chen Dynasty and posts travel vlogs to the channel Mikey Chen. 

Before Fame

He first launched his channels Strictly Dumpling and Beyond Science in October 2013. 

Trivia

A passionate foodie, he often posts his tantalizing meals on his mikexingchen Instagram account where he has more than 410,000 followers. 

Family Life

He has documented travels to Japan, Mongolia, New York, and countless other locations on his self-titled YouTube channel. 

Associated With

Both he and Gabby Valente are YouTubers who have grown popular on YouTube making mystery and paranormal videos.
