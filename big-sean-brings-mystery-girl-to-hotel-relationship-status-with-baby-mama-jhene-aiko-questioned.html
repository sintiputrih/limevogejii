Big Sean Brings Mystery Girl To A Hotel, Sparking Questions About His Relationship Status With Baby

Highlights

  • Big Sean and Jhene Aiko's relationship status is being questioned as Big Sean was seen entering a hotel with a mystery woman.
  • The woman in the photos has not been identified, but it's possible she's a part of Big Sean's team helping him prepare for a performance.
  • While Big Sean has a history of cheating in relationships, he and Aiko have remained together and recently welcomed their son, suggesting they are still in a relationship.

Big Sean and Jhene Aiko seemed to be going strong recently, as they welcomed their first child, a son, earlier this year. But now fan are questioning their relationship status a Big Sean was seen heading into a hotel with a mystery woman, while his partner was nowhere in sight.

Newly taken photo show Big Sean arriving in a lime green hoodie to his hotel in Las Vegas. The rapper tried to conceal his identity, as he pulled the hood over his face, while photographers took photos of the duo.

The woman in the photos has yet to be identified, but she was dressed casually in a jacket and leggings. She had a duffle bag in hand, while Big Sean had a back pack and laptop case, suggesting they’re both staying at the hotel.

Big Sean And Jhene Aiko Had A Baby This Year

While arriving at a hotel with a mystery woman may seem suspicious, there’s nothing to suggest Big Sean is romantically involved with the woman. It’s possible she’s a part of his team is helping him prep for a performance.

Earlier this week, Big Sean showed off his family when he posted his son’s luxurious teething ring – seriously – to Instagram. “My boy throwing Louis Vuitton teething toys around, he different,” Big Sean wrote on his IG Story over top an image of the designer baby toy.

Though Big Sean and Aiko often collaborate together, and even do joint performances, they’re relatively quiet about their relationship. But the rapper couldn’t help but gush about his son’s mother while performing with her earlier this year, acknowledging how much she’s sacrificed to be a working mother.

“It took her so much to get up on this stage, because she’s so hands-on with him,” Big Sean told the crowd. “She really nurtures and takes care of this baby and it took a lot for her to get up on stage.”

Big Sean Has A History Of Cheating In Relationships

While Big Sean and Aiko have addressed speculation that things are amiss with their relationship, the rapper does have a history of cheating on partners. He infamously cheated on this then-fiancé, Glee actress Naya Rivera, with Ariana Grande, whom he went to pursue a relationship with after his engagement ended.

Though Big Sean has been with Aiko since 2016, he’s been accused of stepping out on her multiple times. In 2018, there were rumors that he cheated with Pussycat Dolls singer Nicole Scherzinger, which all parties later denied.

“Sorry, but nothing about you guy’s fan fiction stories are true… I still love you tho,” Aiko tweeted at the time. “the internet is a wild place it’s a whole other reality. bless the internet. amen.”

More recently, in May 2022, a video surfaced allegedly showing Big Sean getting cozy with two unidentified women. However, despite the reports, Sean and Aiko remained together, and went on to welcome their son, Noah, last November. For now, the two still appear very much in a relationship.
