Colby Brock A Famous YouTuber Has Been Diagnosed With Testicular Cancer

Colby Brock, a YouTuber, recently shocked his followers by announcing that he had been diagnosed with testicular cancer. On February 7, 2023, the YouTuber stated on Twitter:

“I went to visit a doctor for some discomfort I was getting in my lower pelvis/left testicle, and after scans/blood work, I was informed I had a lump in my ball that was 95% likely to be cancer.”

In regards to his operation, he stated:

“I went to surgery yesterday to have my cancer ball removed, so the difficult part is finished. I shall know in a few of weeks whether I need more treatment, such as chemotherapy or radiation.”
