Who is Jake Matthews:

Jake Matthews is a famous Football Player. He was born on February 11, 1992 and his birthplace is Missouri City, TX. Jake is also well known as, Offensive tackle who was selected 6th overall in the 2014 NFL Draft by the Atlanta Falcons after a stellar collegiate career at Texas A&M where he was named a First-Team All-American in 2012.

Jake is originated from United States. His cousin Clay Matthews won a Super Bowl Championship in 2011 as a star linebacker with the Green Bay Packers.
