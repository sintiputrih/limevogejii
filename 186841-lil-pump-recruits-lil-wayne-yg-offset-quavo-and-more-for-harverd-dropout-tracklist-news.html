Lil Pump Recruits Lil Wayne, YG, Offset, Quavo &amp; More For Harverd Dropout Tracklist

After a several setbacks & delays, Lil Pump’s long awaited Harverd Dropout album finally drops this Friday. But before it arrives, the “I Love It” rapper decides to share the tracklist with us ahead of time, and there’s some major features on it.

The follow up to 2017’s self titled debut will contain 16 tracks in total and feature guest appearances from Kanye West, Lil Wayne, Lil Uzi Vert, Smokepurpp, Quavo, Offset, YG and 2 Chainz. Meanwhile, production will be handled by the likes of Ronny J, Kanye West, Danny Wolf, Dee Money, Diablo, CBMIX, and others. It’s currently led by the previously head singles “I Love It,” “Rack On Racks,” “Eskeetit” & "Drug Addicts" to name a few.

In addition to the tracklist, Pump also shared the project’s official cover art as well Tuesday evening, which fittingly finds him rocking a purple cap & gown from a graduation. However you'll notice it's not the Harvard color scheme, but the color of lean.
