Choosing the Best Wave Brush

Here are some essential factors to consider before selecting a wave brush:

Material

If you want a long-lasting tool, invest in a wave brush that is durable and effective on your hair. Most brushes have wooden bases and handles, making them easy to grip and use as many times as you want. Moreover, a wooden brush doesn’t break easily, even if you drop it often.

Bristles

A brush with hard, durable bristles will help you achieve fuller waves that stay in place for a long time. While hard doesn’t specify the brush will feel hard to touch, it actually implies that the bristles won’t break off easily and penetrate your hair follicles deeper.  Alternatively, soft bristles are ideal for shorter hair because they don’t aggravate your scalp.

Finally, look for a wave brush with boar bristles that may also help to evenly distribute the oil in your scalp, creating a shiny hairstyle for you every time.

Handle

Invest in a wave brush that comes with a handle. This part is essential if you don’t want to get tired while styling your hair. Since a durable handle offers a firm grip for your hands, you’ll have complete control over your dream hairstyle.

Shape

A wave brush comes in different shapes and sizes. Some are available in a rectangular shape, while others have a curvy structure.

The correct shape depends on your comfort level. We do, however, recommend a curvy brush because it efficiently contours your hair.

How to Use a Wave Brush

There is no complicated technique involved when using a wave brush. Simply use the tool in the direction you want your waves to lay down. For perfect waves, brush towards the shoulder area to distribute your hair oil and maintain the shape of your waves.

If you aim to achieve the best results, brush your hair two times every day and use this tool before taking a bath to remove all the tangles in your hair.
