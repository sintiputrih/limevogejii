Biography

After four seasons with the Kings, he signed with the New Orleans Pelicans in 2013. He was then traded back to the Kings in 2017. He flourished under head coach John Calipari at the University of Memphis. He played one season at the University of Memphis, where he was the only freshman finalist for the 2009 U.S. Basketball Writers of America National Player of the Year Award.
